  • Help

    [Hover to show help]
    • CTRL + click a node to show the modification helper
    • Move a node by dragging the aforementioned modification helper.
    • Resize nodes by moving your mouse to the edges of the modification helper. When your cursor changes, click and move the mouse to change the node's size.
    • Hide the modification helper by clicking it while holding the CTRL key.
    • Add edge handles to a straight line edge by clicking on it.
    • Enable modifications of splines by holding CTRL and clicking on the spline. Treat the blue dots like any other node, e.g. CTRL + Click or drop it.
